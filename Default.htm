Home

Welcome to the webpage of Danish Academic Economists in North America (DAEiNA). We are an organization for Danish economists working or studying in North America.

More details of our organization and its activities can be found under about.

The website also contains a list of recent activities and relevant events in and around the organization.

To see a list of current members go to our members page.

One of the goals of our organization is to provide help and information to Danes interested in studying or working in Economics in North America. Our page of resources contains various relevant documents and links.

DAEiNA can be reached via e-mail at daeina@daeina.net.
