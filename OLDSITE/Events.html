March 23 2013

2013 DAEiNA Meeting Program

A new board was elected at the 2013 DAEiNA meeting. Leaving the board is former chairman Torben G. Andersen and board member Nikolaj A. Harmon. We thank both for their hard work during a successful year where DAEiNA more than tripled its membership and doubled the number of participants at the yearly meeting.


Replacing Nikolaj and Torben is new chairman Tim Bollerslev and Christian Lund. Congratulations to Tim and Christian on their election to the board. Also congratulations to Rasmus Jørgensen who was elected for another term on the board.
